Hierarchy Extraction

  • Looking at a random tree-structure, we can see that greater centrality corresponds to the higher level in the hierarchy

Hierarchy Extraction

  1. Define a suitable hierarchy score for each node
  2. Compare scores for 2 neighboring nodes (in the underlying undirected network)
    1. If ratio of scores is between a lower and an upper threshold
    2. Then, the node with the higher score is higher in the hierarchy
  3. Cutoff eliminates
    1. relations between two highly different nodes
    2. relations where the nodes are too similar (i.e. ratio ~1)
    3. edges to leaf nodes in the hierarchy
